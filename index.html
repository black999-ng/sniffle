Our Services

Services designed for you

Veterinary Services

We provide comprehensive animal health care that keeps your livestock productive and disease-free: Preventive health programs (vaccinations, deworming, biosecurity). Routine and emergency clinical care for cattle, horses, sheep, goats, and poultry. Herd health management tailored for both smallholder and large farms. Mobile veterinary units for on-farm services – bringing care closer to farmers.

Artificial Insemination (AI) and Training

Unlock the genetic potential of your animals through advanced breeding solutions: Artificial Insemination (AI) with superior genetics to boost milk and meat productivity. Estrus synchronization and fertility management. Reproductive biotechnology for herd improvement programs. Farmer and technician training on AI techniques and reproductive health.

Smart Monitoring

Tracking Data for informed decision-making. Data-driven insights on feeding patterns, breeding cycles, and disease prevention strategies.

BioTalks, Consulting and Capacity Building

Live expert sessions on biotechnology and animal health. We empower farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses with knowledge and expertise: Farmer field schools, workshops, and demonstrations. Training of veterinarians, paraveterinarians, and AI technicians. Farm productivity consulting (nutrition, herd management, breeding strategies). Project-based support for NGOs, agribusinesses, and government programs.
